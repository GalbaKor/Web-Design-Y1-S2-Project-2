About us

Welcome to Memoires and Megapixels. We are a photography museum operating from Bristol city centre.
Suitable for anyone and everyone interested in photography, we welcome the curious, the amateur, the professional and more.
We would be thrilled if you are interested in attending any of our upcoming events, including half-term events for children.
What's more, we are always looking for more members to join the museum and provide an open space for members to show off their portfolios.

Events: tours and more

Here at Memoires and Megapixels we are constantly holding events for new exhibits, giving tours of our permanent collection and providing half-term events suitable for kids.
